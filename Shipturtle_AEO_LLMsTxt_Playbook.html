Your agency delivered a 7,533-line raw website scrape ‚Äî navigation menus, image CDN URLs, cookie banners, footer markup, duplicate testimonials, and JavaScript artifacts. The LLMs.txt specification requires a concise, curated markdown file of ~100-200 lines with structured links to your most important pages. The file you received would confuse AI systems rather than help them.

What LLMs.txt Actually Is

A lightweight markdown file at yoursite.com/llms.txt that follows a specific structure: an H1 with your brand name, a blockquote summary, and curated sections with links to your most important pages. Think of it as a librarian's recommended reading list ‚Äî not a complete catalogue of every page. Proposed by Jeremy Howard (Answer.AI) in September 2024.

Current Adoption Reality

844,000+ websites have implemented LLMs.txt including Anthropic, Cloudflare, and Stripe. However, as of mid-2025, OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic have NOT implemented native support in their primary AI products. Semrush testing found zero visits from GPTbot, PerplexityBot, or ClaudeBot. It's a low-risk future-proofing move ‚Äî not your primary AEO strategy.

Update Frequency Guide

Update your LLMs.txt whenever you make meaningful changes. For SaaS products like Shipturtle, that means: immediately for pricing changes or URL changes; within 1 week for new product launches or major features; monthly quick review for dead links; quarterly substantive update to add new high-value content and restructure sections. Total ongoing effort: ~30 minutes per quarter.

‚Ñπ What About llms-full.txt?

Some brands also create an expanded llms-full.txt that includes the actual content of key pages (not just links). This helps AI systems ingest your content in a single request. For Shipturtle, this is low priority ‚Äî focus on deploying the standard llms.txt first, then the on-page AEO work (which delivers 10x more immediate impact), and create the full version in Q2 2026 if resources allow.

Every pillar page needs 5-8 FAQs. Every cluster blog needs 3-5 FAQs. These aren't just for Google's People Also Ask ‚Äî they're the primary way AI systems extract Q&A pairs from your content.

Rules for AEO-optimized FAQs:

Pillar 4: Entity & Topical Authority Building Strategic ‚ñæ

AI systems determine authority based on how comprehensively you cover a topic cluster, not just individual keywords. If your brand targets "multi-vendor marketplace" but ignores foundational related terms, AI won't consider you authoritative.

Entity map Shipturtle must own:

  • Type entities: B2B marketplace, B2C marketplace, C2C marketplace, service marketplace, rental marketplace
  • Operations entities: vendor management, marketplace commission, vendor payouts, order fulfillment
  • Comparison entities: marketplace vs ecommerce store, Shopify Collective vs alternatives, build vs buy marketplace

Critical gaps in your entity coverage (see Content Gaps tab for full details): You're missing definitional pages for B2B marketplace, C2C marketplace, horizontal vs vertical, and multi-vendor vs single-vendor ‚Äî all foundational entities that AI references when evaluating topical authority.

Pillar 5: Content Structure for AI Extraction Ongoing ‚ñæ

AI systems don't read pages top-to-bottom. They extract chunks ‚Äî individual paragraphs, lists, tables, and definitions ‚Äî and evaluate each chunk independently.
