Content Pruning Audit — Industry Marketplaces Pillar

Pillar: Industry Verticals

Pillar Health Summary

This is the largest pillar with 43 child pages — well above the 8–15 recommended cluster size. It has 6 star performers (100+ clicks) led by #71 Health & Wellness (251 clicks), #80 Sustainable Products (212 clicks), and #93 Travel (206 clicks). But it also has 10 zero-traffic pages and 5 cannibalization pairs. The main risk here is content sprawl — too many thin vertical pages diluting each other instead of building deep authority in fewer verticals.

Strategic Recommendation

Consolidate → Deepen → Expand. Rather than covering 30+ industry verticals shallowly, consolidate overlapping verticals (fashion + sustainable clothing + shoes = one fashion hub), deepen the top 15 performers into 3,000+ word definitive guides, and only then consider expansion. The geo-story pages (#103–#106) need the same intent-first treatment as Marketplace Types — editorial titles are killing discoverability.

Audit Philosophy: Intent Before Metrics

Every page evaluated on: 1) Does real search intent exist for this industry vertical? 2) Is the vertical relevant to Shipturtle's ICP? 3) Is underperformance due to content depth, title optimization, or genuinely low demand? Zero-traffic pages are only flagged for redirect when the intent is off-brand or fully covered elsewhere.
